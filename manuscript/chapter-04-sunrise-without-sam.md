# Chapter 4 - Sunrise Without Sam

## Purpose

First meaningful solo hike: Neck Point at sunrise because Sam wants to sleep in.

## Draft Spine

Kevin invites Kelsey because he wants to photograph the sunrise and Sam refuses to leave bed that early. The excuse is practical enough to be safe.

The hike should be quiet, cold, and textured: breath in the dark, damp railings, camera strap, tide smell, the first orange line of light. Kevin explains what he is looking for in the frame. Kelsey realizes that being beside him while he looks for beauty feels different than looking at his finished pictures later.

Nothing happens. That is the point. The restraint makes the next invitation easier.
