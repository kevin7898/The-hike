# Chapter 18 - One Tent

## Purpose

Let Kelsey discover Kevin's tent problem and make the one-tent reality land.

## Draft Spine

Kelsey helps Kevin roll out his tent before they set up hers. She is the one who notices the smell first, then the mold.

Let the scene be practical before it is charged. Kevin is annoyed at himself. Kelsey teases him. The ruined tent gets packed away.

Only after the practical problem is solved should the implication arrive: one tent, one night, then another.

Neither of them needs to say it immediately.
