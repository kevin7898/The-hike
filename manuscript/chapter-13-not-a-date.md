# Chapter 13 - Not A Date

## Purpose

Give the story a casual bridge before the trip.

## Draft Spine

Kelsey and Kevin meet in a context that is pointedly not a date. Coffee, gear shopping, or a short walk works.

The trick is that both of them know the denial is getting thin. Kevin does not name it. Kelsey almost wants him to.

End with Kelsey understanding that she does not necessarily want a relationship. She wants access, heat, trust, and a way to have those things without hurting Sam.
