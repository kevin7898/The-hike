# Chapter 12 - After The Hike

`Running ten late. Showering before pickup because I am merciful.`

"It has drainage issues."

"It means not bad."

"You are a very literal man."

"Sam says that until she needs something assembled."

Kelsey handed the camera back. Their fingers brushed. This time she did not pretend not to notice. Neither did he.

The parking lot appeared too soon.

"How was the hike?" Sam asked.

"Muddy," Kevin said.

"Flirtatious," Kelsey almost said, and choked on coffee instead.

Sam's eyes sharpened with delight. "Interesting."

"It was mud," Kelsey said.

"Famously erotic."

Kevin looked into his cup like a man choosing survival.
