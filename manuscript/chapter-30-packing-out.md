# Chapter 30 - Packing Out

## Purpose

Let the hike down be its own emotional chapter.

## Draft Spine

Breaking camp should take time: folding, checking, loading, realizing what smells like smoke and what still feels damp.

The hike down is quieter than the hike in. Kelsey is sore, happy, uncertain, and unwilling to pretend this was nothing.

Kevin gives her space without becoming distant.

End at the truck, where the ordinary world returns too suddenly.
