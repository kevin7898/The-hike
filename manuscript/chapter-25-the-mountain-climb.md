# Chapter 25 - The Mountain Climb

## Purpose

Let Sam's text change Kelsey's confidence on the trail.

## Draft Spine

After the text, Kelsey climbs differently. Not recklessly, not performatively, but with less apology in her body.

The trail gets steeper. Sweat matters. Breath matters. Pack straps, shirt gaps, water breaks, and Kevin's stolen glances all matter.

Kelsey should give him one or two views on purpose and know exactly what she is doing.

End just before the summit opens.
