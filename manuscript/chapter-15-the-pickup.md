# Chapter 15 - The Pickup

## Purpose

Start the camping trip slowly enough that the reader feels the weight of Sam stepping back.

## Draft Spine

Kevin picks Kelsey up with the truck already loaded and still finds room to be awkward about one small thing: where her pack should go, whether she needs coffee, whether Sam texted her.

Kelsey has packed differently because of Sam. That should sit in the scene without being overexplained.

The drive should carry nervous humor, practical gear talk, and silence that means more than it should. Kelsey does not want to call it a date. Kevin does not ask her to.

End with the trailhead and the first lift of the packs.
