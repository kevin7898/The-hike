# Chapter 8 - The Earrings Conversation

## Purpose

Kelsey learns enough about Sam and Kevin's openness to stop treating her curiosity as impossible.

## Draft Spine

Kelsey notices Sam's earrings over coffee. She knows enough internet shorthand to ask, but not enough to sound casual.

Sam answers in detail because hiding would make it weirder. She explains openness, jealousy, rules, freedom, and how Kevin fits inside all of it. She should be warm but not sanitized.

Kelsey asks questions that are partly about Sam and partly about Kevin, though she is not ready to admit the second part.

Seed Sam/Kelsey bi tension here: Kelsey notices Sam's mouth around a teasing answer, then gets annoyed at herself for noticing.
