# Chapter 31 - The Drive Home

## Purpose

Start naming the FWB pivot without fully resolving it.

## Draft Spine

The truck smells like damp gear, sunscreen, snacks, smoke, and tired bodies. Kelsey watches the island road unwind and feels the trip turning into memory before she is ready.

She finally says they should not pretend it was casual.

Kevin agrees.

Then she says she also does not want to make it a relationship just because it was intense.

Kevin does not flinch.

That is the first clean step toward the ending.
