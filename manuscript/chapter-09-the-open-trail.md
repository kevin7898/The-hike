# Chapter 9 - The Open Trail

## Purpose

First solo hike after Kelsey understands the door is not locked.

## Draft Spine

Kevin and Kelsey hike at sunrise again. The air between them has changed because Kelsey knows more now.

Kevin does not push. That matters. Kelsey watches him not pushing and finds it almost more dangerous than flirting would be.

They talk about Sam only enough to keep honesty in the room. The chapter should build tension through pauses, hand placement, trail help, and how Kelsey becomes aware of walking in front of him.
