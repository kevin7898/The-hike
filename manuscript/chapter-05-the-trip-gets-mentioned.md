# Chapter 5 - The Trip Gets Mentioned

## Purpose

Bring in the future camping trip while keeping Kelsey mostly in friendship mode.

## Draft Spine

On a group hike, Sam mentions that she and Kevin have been talking about an overnight trip. Maybe two nights if the weather holds.

Kelsey reacts as a hiker first. She asks about route, elevation, water, weather, and whether Sam is actually going to enjoy carrying a pack. The attraction should remain background static.

End with Kelsey at home checking gear she does not yet know she will need for a different version of the trip.
