# Chapter 20 - Dinner With The Tent Behind Them

## Purpose

Slow the night down before the first major threshold.

## Draft Spine

Dinner should be simple: stove, packets, snacks, water, laughter at something too small to matter anywhere else. The tent sits behind them like a fourth presence.

Kevin and Kelsey talk more honestly here, but not about labels. What do they owe Sam? What did Sam actually make room for? What happens if they want what both of them are pretending not to have planned?

By the end of dinner, the question is no longer whether something might happen. It is whether Kelsey is ready to choose it.
