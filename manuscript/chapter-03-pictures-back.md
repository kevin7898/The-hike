# Chapter 3 - Pictures Back

## Purpose

Let Kelsey send Kevin her own photos and begin wanting his attention without naming the want.

## Draft Spine

Kelsey sends a trail picture back. She almost deletes it first because it is not as good as his, then sends it anyway.

Kevin does not flatter her lazily. He notices something specific: framing, weather, the way she caught the trail bending out of sight. That specificity matters more than praise.

Sam remains present in the chapter through texts, plans, and an evening at home with Kevin. Kelsey should not feel like a replacement. The reader should feel the triangle forming while the characters can still pretend it is ordinary friendship.
