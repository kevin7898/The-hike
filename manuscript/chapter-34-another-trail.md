# Chapter 34 - Another Trail

## Purpose

End by proving the arrangement can continue.

## Draft Spine

Kevin and Kelsey meet for another hike after the trip and after the conversations. This time the tension has a name.

Kelsey says friends with benefits is not a downgrade if the friends part is real.

Kevin agrees.

Sam texts before they leave. Something teasing, filthy, and affectionate enough to prove she is still present.

Kelsey no longer feels like she is sneaking into someone else's life. She is stepping into a chosen arrangement with eyes open.

On the trail, Kevin offers his hand over a rock she does not need help with.

Kelsey takes it anyway.

The ending should not promise forever. It should promise honesty, appetite, friendship, and the next trail.
