# Chapter 2 - No Reason To Reread

## Purpose

Keep the early connection innocent while showing Kelsey rereading texts anyway.

## Draft Spine

Kelsey tells herself there is no reason to reread Kevin's messages. They are about photos, weather, trails, and whether the morning light over Hammond Bay was worth stepping outside before coffee.

That is all.

So she rereads them.

Nothing in the chapter should make her feel guilty yet. Her private interest is still deniable: Kevin is easy to talk to, Sam loves him, and Kelsey likes having a friend who notices the same small outdoor details she does. Use waterfront walks, work interruptions, and quiet evenings on her deck.
