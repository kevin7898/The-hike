# Chapter 16 - Trail In

## Purpose

Make the hike itself physical before the story becomes sexual.

## Draft Spine

The trail in should be all body logistics: pack weight, sweat under straps, gravel under boots, hands offered over roots, Kelsey letting Kevin help with obstacles she could manage alone.

Kevin notices without making a speech out of noticing. Kelsey notices him noticing.

The chapter should keep the erotic pressure in movement. Who walks first? Who gets the view? Who stops for water? Who pretends the pause is only about the climb?

End with the first clear view of the lake.
