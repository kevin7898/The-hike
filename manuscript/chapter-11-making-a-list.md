# Chapter 11 - Making A List

## Purpose

Plan the camping trip and vary Kevin/Kelsey dialogue.

## Draft Spine

Gear lists become a safe way to talk often. Kelsey asks about pack weight, socks, food, layers, water filtering, and whether Sam will complain the whole way up.

Kevin answers practically, then occasionally lets something warmer slip through.

The flirting should still be deniable. Kelsey should feel the difference between useful advice and attention.
