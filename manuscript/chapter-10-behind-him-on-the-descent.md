# Chapter 10 - Behind Him On The Descent

## Purpose

Use a group hike to keep Sam present while Kelsey becomes more aware of Kevin physically.

## Draft Spine

Kevin leads on the way down. Kelsey notices his shoulders, his pace, his hand reaching back when the trail gets rough.

Sam notices more than she says.

The lunch break should have actual group rhythm: jokes, food, water, sore legs, shared sunscreen, someone dropping something into the dirt. This chapter should not feel like two people flirting while Sam disappears. Sam is there, and that is what makes the tension complicated.
