- 34 chapters across four acts.
- Slow burn through repeated hikes and texts.
- Sam and Kelsey are both bi, but Sam is more settled in it.
- Kevin/Kelsey pivots away from romance and toward friends with benefits.
- First major Kevin/Kelsey physical threshold remains on the camping trip.

## Act 1 - Friendship And Permission

More hiking and everyday texts. Kelsey begins sharing her own pictures with Kevin. Include a Sam/Kevin interlude so their relationship stays alive on-page.

### Chapter 5 - The Trip Gets Mentioned

## Act 4 - What This Is

Chapters 30-34 finish the story the Grok chat left unfinished.
